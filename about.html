Our Story

About the Wellbeing Hub

Founded in 2020, we're a community-driven health centre dedicated to making wellbeing accessible, affordable, and stigma-free for everyone in Dagenham.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and families by providing accessible, high-quality mental health, wellbeing, and community support services without judgment or barriers.

🌟

Our Vision

A thriving community where every person has access to the support they need to achieve health, happiness, and fulfillment.

🤝

Our Values

Compassion, inclusion, respect, and integrity guide every decision. We meet people where they are, without judgment or barriers to access.

🧭

Our Approach

We combine evidence-based services with community wisdom. We listen, we learn, and we evolve based on what our community needs.
